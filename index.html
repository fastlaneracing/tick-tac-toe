Safety first

Friendly does not mean unsupervised.

Every petting experience is led by trained keepers, sized for the animal, and limited to small groups. Guests receive clear instructions before every encounter.

  • Closed-toe shoes are recommended on the boardwalk.
  • Children must stay with an adult during encounters.
  • Only park-provided food may be offered to animals.
  • Handwashing stations are placed at every animal contact area.
